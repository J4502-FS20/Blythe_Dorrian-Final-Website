Need to call someone?

Hello! If you are in need of someone to talk to, you have come to the right place. There are some national hotline numbers that can be accessed from anywhere in the world.

There are places people can go to for help. The hardest step is looking to find something.

National contacts:

The 24 Hour Crisis Hotline is (844)-942-2459.

The Mental Health Hotline's phone number is 1-800-662-4357 (HELP)
